My top 3 movies

A list of the 3 best movies I have watched

The Imitation Game

This movie is about the life of the man who invented the first computer, in order to decrypt secret messages of the german army during WWII

Inception

This Christopher Nolan's classic is amazing just for the fact that it messes with your mind, making you wait for the end to see what's actually real

On Time

I particularly enjoyed this movie thanks to its extremely original plot
